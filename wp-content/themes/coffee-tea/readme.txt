== Description ==

Coffee Tea is a vibrant WordPress theme designed to perfectly cater to tea and coffee shops, cafes, and specialty beverage establishments. With a modern and visually appealing layout, this theme offers a sophisticated and inviting look that is sure to attract customers. Its clean and organized design makes it easy to showcase a wide array of offerings, from artisan teas and gourmet coffees to delicious pastries and snacks. The theme’s homepage features customizable sections where you can highlight your best-selling items, special promotions, and new arrivals, making it an excellent tool for drawing attention to your signature drinks like lattes, cappuccinos, and matcha teas. The layout is intuitively structured to enhance user experience, with clearly defined areas for menu items, customer reviews, and contact information. This ensures that visitors can easily navigate your site and find the information they need, whether they are looking for a cozy local cafe for their morning espresso, a tea house offering unique tea blends, or a place to enjoy a relaxing cup of herbal tea. One of the key benefits of this theme is its high degree of customization. You can effortlessly adjust the color scheme, fonts, and layout to match your brand’s identity, creating a cohesive and professional online presence.

== Installation ==
	
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
